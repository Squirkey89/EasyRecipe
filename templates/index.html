Welcome to Easy Recipes. You'll find simple, step-by-step recipes that you can make yourself.

WHAT WE DO?

We want to create a library of tasty dishes, and want to show you how easy it is to prepare these meals yourself.

Using this website, you can discover which dishes to prepare for today, tomorrow, the rest of the week, and on special occasions.

This website will be community-driven. Share your family recipes that your parents and grandparents taught you that will be used for generations to come.

If you would like to add a recipe, just go to the sign up page and after you are signed up, you can add recipes.

All the recipes you have created will appear on the recipe page, where you can browse them and those created by others.
